Our approach

A straight line from idea to launch.

The name is the philosophy. Linear — a clear, direct path with no wasted motion. Arc — the full journey we carry a project across, from first sketch to shipped product.

We're a senior team that takes ownership of the technical hard parts. No layers of account managers, no template factories — just engineers who care about clean code, reliable integrations, and work that holds up under real use.

1

Discover

We dig into the problem, the platforms, and the constraints before writing a line of code.

2

Architect

A clear build plan and integration map, so you know exactly what's being built and why.

3

Build

Tight iteration, visible progress, and code written to last beyond the launch.

4

Launch & support

We ship it, connect it, and stay close while it settles into real-world use.
